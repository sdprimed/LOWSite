Refunds will only be issued if wine is damaged or lost. You must file a claim with the shipper and provide proof of claim.

Your satisfaction is our first priority. If for any reason there is an issue with your order, please contact Customer Care at info@littleoakswinery.com within 30 days of receipt of your order for assistance.
