As a member of our Wine Club you will be able to enjoy our wines year round with three of our finest red wines arriving at your door every quarter. These are hand selected by the winemaker for our members.

You will also have access to exclusive preferential pricing on our products. As a member you will receive:

  • 20% off bottles
  • 25% of cases of wine through our online store

You will also receive exclusive access to limited quantity varietals and our newest releases before they are offered to the general public. Wine Club members can choose to have their wine delivered to them or be picked up at our warehouse. When a tasting room is available, our members will receive 2 free tastings with every visit.

Wanna join? Of course you do! So go to our secure ecommerce site Wine Club and sign up.

Please email wineclub@littleoakswinery.com for more information.
