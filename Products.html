Our Wines

2013 Viognier

19 cases produced.

Our 2013 Viognier is out of Buddenhagen Vineyards in north San Diego County. As with most Viognier, the yield is quite low producing a high quality grape. Our Viognier has a floral aroma with a crisp finish that is sure to leave you refreshed on even the warmest days. This vintage was slightly oaked giving the wine a warm golden hue, a fuller body, and nice complexity. Our 2013 Viognier would pair well with shellfish and fish in a white sauce or that is lightly spiced with coriander. Our Viognier is very popular and with limited supply will go fast.

100% Viognier

2014 Viognier

AWARDS:
2015 TOAST OF THE COAST | SILVER MEDAL

20 cases produced.

Our award winning 2014 Viognier is out of Buddenhagen Vineyards in north San Diego County. As with the 2013 vintage, the yield is quite low producing a high quality grape. It is a highly aromatic wine with tropical fruits soaring from the glass with hints of apricot, pear and citrus. The finish is crisp, clean, and refreshing. Our 2014 vintage is a true expression of the classic Viognier character. Our Viognier would pair well with lobster, soft cheeses, and nuts. It is very popular and with limited supply it will go fast.

100% Viognier

Red Wine Carignane Cabernet

42 cases produced.

We call this our Cab-Cari blend. While our Carignane has plenty or aroma and flavor we decided to give it a little extra oomph and blend it with our Cabernet Sauvignon to give the wine extra body and depth. On the palette the Carignane hits first with darks fruits and the Cabernet Sauvignon provide the nice finish of black currant and smoky vanilla. This blend is an easily drinkable and very versatile with food, it can be paired with hard cheeses to a braised lamb with onions and mushrooms.

67% Carignane, 32% Cabernet Sauvignon, 1% Syrah

2013 Sangiovese

73 cases produced.

Our Sangiovese grapes are harvest at their ideal ripeness when the berries are at the perfect sweetness and ideal reddish blue color. The grapes are from Buddenhagen Vineyards in north San Diego County and aged in Hungarian Oak barrels for over 14 months. Our Sangiovese shares some of its characteristics with its Italian cousin; it has a relatively high acidity and medium tannins. The aroma has scents of tart cherry, fig, and leather. Our Sangiovese given its high acidity levels would pair excellently with tomatoes and herbs, a perfect match for a margarita style pizza.

100% Sangiovese

2013 Malbec

33 cases produced.

Our Malbec grapes are out of Buddenhagen Vineyards in north San Diego County. The young vines produce a high quality black skinned grape. It was aged in Hungarian Oak barrels for over 14 months giving the wine great body and depth. Our Malbec has a deep garnet color and the aroma is full of purple plums and violets. Our Malbec is bold and has a robust earthiness appeal with medium tannins; it would pair well with a grilled meat such as chicken or ribs and savory barbeque sauce.

100% Malbec

2013 Syrah

71 cases produced.

Our Syrah grapes are out of Sohegian Vineyard in north San Diego County from 10 year old vines. As with most Syrah varietals, our Syrah has wonderful aging properties. Our Syrah was aged in Hungarian Oak barrels for over 14 months to give it a bold color and high tannins. Our Syrah is full of blackberry and sweet tobacco. Overall, it is a dramatic wine with weight, tannins, and tremendous complexity. Given the structure of our Syrah, it would pair well with a barbeque or roasted pork with spices such as cumin and black pepper.

100% Syrah

2013 Barbera

56 cases produced.

Our Barbera grapes are out of Hamilton Pointe Vineyards on the Santa Rosa Plateau. The wine has a deep dark purple color, distinctive of the Barbera varietal. Our Barbara was aged in Hungarian Oak barrels for over 14 months. The nose is rich in dark cherries and spice and finished with hints of dandelion and dark berries. It is nicely balanced and is easy to drink. The wine would pair well with many different foods including roast pork, Italian sausage, tomatoes, garlic, and olives.

100% Barbera

2013 Red Wine Merlot Syrah

25 cases produced.

We call this our 50/50 blend. This special mix of Merlot and Syrah provides a full body wined with aromas of darks berries and plums. The Merlot with medium tannins blending with the higher tannins of the Syrah creates an exquisite balance in the wine. This blend, even though a bit more robust, is sure to be a crowd pleaser. This wine can be paired with many different foods including caramelized vegetables, roast turkey, and steak.

50% Merlot, 50% Syrah

2013 Red Wine Malbec Syrah

26 cases produced.

Our Malbec-Syrah blend is the blend we are most excited about. This blend is unique in that the two wines were combined in Hungarian Oak barrels and allowed to age in the together for over 14 months. The color is a beautiful dark jeweled tone purple with aromas of licorice, blackberry, and pepper. The two wines match evenly in body and structure which leaving a lasting impression on the palate and eager for the next sip. Being a blend of two big robust wines it would pair best with a nice rib eye roast with onions and blue cheese.

60% Malbec, 40% Syrah
