History: Under Construction

"It all started in 1993 when I moved into a house in Palo Alto. There was a beautiful old apricot tree in the backyard, a remnant of the fruit groves that used to blanket the Santa Clara Valley in the first half of the 20th century."   Rich Sportsman
In starting up Little Oaks Winery, we have joined a group of over 100 commercial wineries now operating in the San Diego area. Historically, San Diego was the first area to produce wine in Alta California.
Starting in 2015, we are releasing our first 2013 vintages, ranging from a pleasant light Viognier to a very sultry Malbec, all made from grapes in the greater San Diego area.
